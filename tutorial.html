Posing

Whether at a football game, homecoming pictures session, or in the school hallway, a 14 year-old girl is always ready to strike a pose and smile for the camera. The first step to posing like a 14 year-old girl is making sure you are camera ready 24/7. Next, strike a silly pose. Whether that is popping your leg up, abnormal hand gestures, or sticking out your tongue is up to you. However, most 14 year-old girls pick one pose that becomes their signature.
