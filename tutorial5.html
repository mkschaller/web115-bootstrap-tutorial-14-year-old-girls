Homecoming Spirit Week

Is it homecoming week already? Homecoming week is a week during October that 14-year-old girls wait all year for. It is a week that they get to dress up for class as crazily as they wish. If the theme was, for example, dress green, a 14 year-old girl would wear every piece of green clothing she owned. If she owned 5 green t-shirts, she'd pile them all on! Own green eyeshadow? Paint your face with it! The crazier, the better.
